# Branding Decisions

The full naming journey. What we explored, what we rejected and why, and where the decision currently sits.

---

## Brand register (locked)

These were the four decisions made before generating any names. They define what we're looking for.

| Decision | Choice |
| --- | --- |
| Brand family | Fully independent brand. Not Nameless. Not Outpace. Looks like a real software company from day one. |
| Personality | Elite & performance. Athlete-as-pro positioning. Data, precision, edge. Not gritty bro-Hyrox. Not soft wellness. |
| Category scope | Hybrid-athlete framing. Hyrox-relevant but not Hyrox-explicit. Keeps door open for CrossFit-adjacent and tactical. |
| Naming style | English (or English-adopted) words with hidden meaning. Modern, divine, real, elevated. |

---

## Round 1: Greek/Latin philosophical concepts (rejected as a direction)

Names tried: **Arete**, **Kairos**, **Threshold**, **Crucible**, **Axiom**, **Praxis**, **Stoa**.

**Why rejected:** Drew didn't connect with the classical-philosophy register. The hidden meanings were too academic; the names felt earned but not lived-in.

---

## Round 2: English with hidden meaning (rejected as a direction, useful for context)

Names tried: **Plumb**, **Carry**, **Verge**, **Crest**, **Mantle**.

Strongest of this round was **Carry** (literal Hyrox carries + what a coach does + what you carry into the race), but Drew wanted something ending in A.

---

## Round 3: Ending in A, English-feeling, hidden meaning

Names tried: **Delta**, **Arena**, **Aura**, **Strata**, **Saga**.

Drew picked **Aura**. We ran diligence — see "Killed" section below.

---

## Round 4: 10 more options ending in A

Generated to broaden the pool after Aura was killed:

| Name | Surface meaning | Hidden meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tessera | A small tile | One tile in a Roman mosaic. Each workout is a tessera. The plan is the mosaic. |
| Cadenza | A musical term | The moment in a concerto when the orchestra falls silent and the soloist plays alone. Race day. |
| Volta | A turn | In poetry, the volta is the line where the argument changes direction. The pivot in training. |
| Quanta | Plural of quantum | Smallest indivisible units. Training is quanta of effort accumulated. |
| Vita | Life | Root of vitamin, vital, vitality. The animating force. (Risk: contested in food/health brands.) |
| Era | A period of time | Athletes have eras — the build, the breakthrough, the comeback. (Risk: very common.) |
| Persona | Identity | Latin for "mask" — *per-sonare*, "to sound through." The self you put on for competition. |
| Astra | Stars | *Per aspera ad astra* — through hardship, to the stars. |
| Strada | A road | Each athlete's path, walked mile by mile. |
| Vista | A view | The long view a coach takes — the panorama earned by climbing. |

Strongest of this round on **hidden meaning**: Tessera, Cadenza, Volta. Strongest on **modern SaaS feel**: Quanta, Era. Strongest on **clearance** (low brand competition): Tessera, Cadenza.

## Round 5: Periodic table / elemental direction

No major periodic table elements end in A — they almost all end in -um, -on, or -ine. But elemental-feeling words ending in A are rich.

| Name | What it is | Hidden meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Plasma | Fourth state of matter | Also blood plasma — the medium that carries performance through the body. Two layers in one word. |
| Magma | Molten rock beneath surface | Greek for "kneaded mass." Latent power before eruption. Off-season metaphor. |
| Theta | Greek letter | Theta brain waves = deep focus / flow state. Athletes train to access theta in competition. |
| Gamma | Greek letter | Gamma waves = peak cognitive performance. Plus gamma radiation (highest-energy EM). |
| Alumina | Aluminum oxide | Natural form of sapphire and ruby. Second-hardest mineral after diamond. |
| Titania | Titanium dioxide | White pigment + Shakespeare's fairy queen + moon of Uranus. |
| Silica | Silicon dioxide | Foundation of glass, sand, and silicon chips. |
| Aqua | Water (Latin) | Original element of endurance. Heavily used in branding. |
| Terra | Earth (Latin) | Foundation, ground. (Risk: Terra blockchain crashed; some baggage.) |
| Mica | Sheet silicate mineral | Forms in transparent layers — geological time made visible. Periodization metaphor. |

**Helium specifically** was raised and rejected: doesn't end in A, owned by the [Helium Network](https://www.helium.com/) crypto company at scale, "balloon voice" cultural association undercuts elite positioning, and "lightness" connotation fights against heavy-lift hybrid-athlete identity. The Helios root (the sun god, and helium was found in the sun's spectrum before Earth) is beautiful but buried under all of that.

## Killed — full diligence

### Aura — killed

Drew's first pick. Diligence revealed:

- **Six existing Aura fitness apps as of April 2026:** [Aura Fitness Club](https://mwm.ai/apps/aura-fitness-club-app/6757749162) (launched January 2026), [AURA FITNESS HUB](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.aurafitnesshub.app) (updated April 2026), [Aura Training Club](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/aura-training-club/id6760314896), [AuraFIT](https://aurafitness.us/), [AuraFit emotion-aware AI fitness app](https://theaurafit.site/), [AURA Devices](https://auradevices.io/) for body composition.
- **[Aura Health](https://www.aurahealth.io/)** — 7M-user Apple-award-winning meditation/sleep/CBT app, founded 2017, San Francisco. Owns the wellness/health "Aura" mindshare entirely.
- **Phonetic clash with [Oura Ring](https://ouraring.com/)** — the dominant smart ring in the category. Many target athletes already wear Oura. Plus a knockoff calling itself ["Aura Ring"](https://auraringus.com/) compounds the confusion.
- **aura.com** owned by Aura Identity Protection (Robert Downey Jr. ads).
- **Trademark fights** in Class 41 (fitness) and Class 9 (software) effectively guaranteed.
- **Brand-fit problem:** "Aura" pulls toward soft glows, gradients, ethereal energy — opposite of elite-performance design language.

**Verdict:** Killed.

### Mantra — killed

Drew's second lean. Diligence revealed:

- **[Mantra.fit](https://mantra.fit/individuals/)** — 10-year-old personalized fitness, nutrition, and coaching platform. Direct competitor in the same space. Owns mantra.fit and operates an active corporate wellness arm.
- **[MantraCare](https://mantra.care/)** — global AI wellness platform, 40+ countries, 15,000+ experts, mental health + fitness + nutrition + MSK. Enterprise-scale incumbent.
- **Long tail of Mantra fitness apps:** [Mantra Fitness App](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/mantra-fitness-app/id1670975620), [MantraFit on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.mantracare.mantra.fit), [MyFitMantra](https://myfitmantra.com/), [Get Fit Mantra](https://apps.apple.com/is/app/get-fit-mantra/id6550900870), [The Mantra Co.](https://themantraco.com/) (1M+ wellness community), [Mantra Health](https://play.google.com/store/apps/developer?id=Mantra+Care), [Fitness Mantra](http://tech-mantra.co.in/FitnessMantra.html), [Mantra Fitness Pilates studios](https://www.mantrafitness.com/faq) in Florida and California.
- **Beyond fitness:** [Mantra Group](https://mantragroup.com/) (AI revenue SaaS), [Mantra Tec](https://www.mantratec.com/) (biometric security), [OrangeMantra](https://www.orangemantra.com/), [ROI Mantra](https://www.roimantra.com/), [MANTRA DAO](https://app.mantradao.com/) (DeFi).
- **Domains gone:** [mantra.com](http://www.mantra.com/) registered, mantra.fit and mantra.care belong to direct competitors, mantra.app priced as premium.
- **Brand-fit problem:** "Mantra" surface association is yoga, meditation, mindfulness, soft fitness. Hyrox coaches hear "Mantra" and picture a yoga studio, not a sled lane. The hidden meaning (Sanskrit "tool that shapes the mind through repetition") is real, but the surface fights the audience.

**Verdict:** Killed.

### Helium — killed

- Doesn't end in A (violates Drew's own rule).
- [Helium Network](https://www.helium.com/) is a billion-dollar crypto/wireless IoT company that owns helium.com and the trademark.
- "Balloon voice" cultural association is comedic, not elite.
- "Lightness" connotation fights heavy-lift identity.

**Verdict:** Killed.

---

## Pattern observation

Drew has been pulled toward names with a quiet, mystical, presence-coded energy — Aura and Mantra both. That's a real signal about the emotional register he wants. The problem: both words are deeply colonized by the wellness/meditation/yoga industry, which is exactly the category his buyer (a Hyrox coach) is *not* in.

The path forward: find a name with the same emotional register that the wellness industry hasn't already claimed.

---

## Current shortlist (open decision)

These four are the strongest candidates to clear diligence and hit the brand register:

### Anima
- **Surface:** Latin/Jungian root meaning the animating force, the breath, the soul.
- **Hidden:** In Jung, the anima is the inner self that drives outward action — the coach-athlete relationship in a single word.
- **Why it works:** Quiet cousin of Aura with the same energy/presence feeling. Uncrowded in fitness. Modern feel.
- **What needs checking:** Domain (anima.app, getanima.com), trademark in Class 41 and Class 9, fitness-app competition.

### Tessera
- **Surface:** A small tile.
- **Hidden:** One tile in a Roman mosaic. Each workout is a tessera. The training plan is the mosaic. The athlete only sees the picture in retrospect.
- **Why it works:** Beautiful metaphor that does product work. Almost no competition in fitness. Wordmark would design beautifully (tile geometry as logo system).
- **What needs checking:** Domain availability, trademark.

### Plasma
- **Surface:** Fourth state of matter.
- **Hidden:** Most energetic state, plus blood plasma (literal performance medium).
- **Why it works:** Modern, scientific, athletic. Surface meaning, hidden meaning, and product thesis all point at the same idea — the highest-energy state of an athlete in motion.
- **What needs checking:** Plasma is used in software (KDE Plasma), fitness brand competition, trademark in athletic class.

### Theta
- **Surface:** Greek letter.
- **Hidden:** Theta brain waves are the rhythm of deep focus and flow state. Coaches train athletes their entire careers to access theta during competition.
- **Why it works:** Modern SaaS feel. Coaches who know neuroscience nod immediately. Almost no fitness brand competition.
- **What needs checking:** Domain, trademark, broader brand competition.

---

## The recommendation

**Anima** if Drew wants to keep the energy/presence feeling that pulled him toward Aura and Mantra in the first place — same emotional register, but in a word the wellness industry hasn't claimed yet.

**Tessera** is the strongest dark horse — the metaphor is the most honest description of what coaches actually do, and the brand competition is effectively zero.

**Plasma** is the strongest pure-product pick — the wordmark would design hardest-edged, and the meaning aligns perfectly with hybrid endurance.

**Theta** is the most modern-SaaS feeling and the most "coaches in the know will get it."

---

## Next decision

Run the same diligence pass (domain, trademark, fitness-app competition) on the shortlist before committing. Recommend starting with **Anima** since it's closest to the emotional register Drew has been pulled toward all along.
