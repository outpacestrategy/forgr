The wedge

Hybrid programming isn't endurance plus strength. It's a constraint problem.

Running compromises strength adaptation. Heavy lifting compromises run quality. Station work eats both. The coach's job is to balance all of it inside a week without overtraining the athlete. Every existing tool forces a choice — and breaks at the boundary. We don't.

Honest fit check

Forgr is not for everyone.

Forgr makes you faster, not optional.

Forgr is for you if...

  • You coach 15 to 60 Hyrox or hybrid athletes and you're losing an hour a day to admin.
  • Your athletes show up wearing five different watch brands and you're tired of asking them to screenshot data.
  • You want AI to draft next week, not write the whole season.

Forgr is not for you if...

  • You're an athlete looking for a self-coached plan. (Try Edge or RoxHype.)
  • You sell pre-built programs to thousands of buyers. (FITR's storefront is better at that.)
  • You coach general strength or CrossFit and Hyrox is a small slice.

FAQ

Predictable questions, honest answers.

When does it launch?

We're rolling out to a small group of design-partner coaches first, then expanding to early-access waitlist signups over the following weeks. We're prioritizing depth with a few coaches over surface area with many.

What does it integrate with at launch?

Garmin Connect and Strava at launch — between them, that covers the most common wearable data today. Apple Watch (HealthKit) ships next, followed by Coros, Polar, Whoop, and Amazfit post-launch. Stripe handles billing.

How is this different from FITR / Edge / Runna?

FITR is the official Hyrox coach software but built for selling pre-made programs at scale, not for high-touch hybrid coaching. Edge and Runna are athlete apps — coaches don't pay for them, athletes do. Forgr is built for coaches who run a roster, ingest wearable data, and adjust plans week to week.

Who's behind this?

Drew Amunategui — Outpace Strategy Group and Nameless Run Club / Nameless Classic. Building solo through alpha; will hire after the first paying-coach milestone.

Can my athletes use this without a coach?

Not in v1. Forgr is built around the coach-athlete relationship — the athlete app is the white-labeled extension of what the coach sets up. If you're an athlete looking for a self-coached app, Edge and Runna are good options.
